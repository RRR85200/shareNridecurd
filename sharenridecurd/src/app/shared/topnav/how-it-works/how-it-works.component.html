How It works


  • UCM Mules can register as both Rider and Driver or Just as a Rider.
  • Students or any UCM Mule Drivers willing to share their ride can posts the availbility with ride details.
  • Users can Login and see for the availbility of rides
  • If no suitable ride is found create a costumn ride.
  • Riders can use coupons to get discounts on each ride
  • Once the ride is ended users can see their Ride History and keep track of their expenses
